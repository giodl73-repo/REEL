# Role: Platform and Audience

## Focus

Viewer context, device, aspect ratio, captions, hook speed, accessibility, and
export target fit.

## Review questions

- Is the intended platform explicit: phone, desktop, YouTube, social feed, deck,
  installation, or game page?
- Can the video be understood on a phone without sound?
- Are aspect ratio, caption size, and first-three-second hook specified?
- Does the export plan include the cuts the audience actually needs?
