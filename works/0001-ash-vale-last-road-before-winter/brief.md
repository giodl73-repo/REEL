# Brief: Ash Vale - Last Road Before Winter

## Assignment

Create a 45-60 second trailer-style animated scenario video for BANISH's Ash
Vale pressure slice.

## Source scenario

- Repo: BANISH
- Scenario id: `scenario:banish:ash-vale-last-road-before-winter`
- Continuity event: `event:banish:ash-vale-forge-winter-choice`
- Source commit: `3f20886eaaae3657562a010d5bdbc6316e1f6fbb`

## Purpose

Show the playable promise: a moving settlement survives winter by deciding what
to pack, where to move, what to anchor, and what loss becomes memory.

## Audience

- Portfolio reviewer deciding whether REEL can adapt Games Design scenarios.
- Game-design collaborator who needs a quick visual understanding of BANISH's
  Pilgrim Loss loop.
- Phone-first viewer who may watch without sound.

## Format and style

- Format: `trailer`
- Primary style: `isometric-game`
- Alternate style: `storyboard-animatic`

## Required beats

1. Establish Ash Vale before winter.
2. Name the forge choice: Hillkiln, Riverford, or split maintenance.
3. Show the Pilgrim Loss verbs: prepare, migrate, settle, lose visibly, carry
   forward.
4. End on the design thesis: loss is not hidden failure; it becomes memory.

## Non-goals

- Do not invent new BANISH canon.
- Do not imply final art direction.
- Do not require cinematic AI provider integration.
- Do not render final game UI.
