# Script

## Voiceover

Ash Vale has one road left before winter.

Hillkiln can keep the forge, but the road is failing. Riverford can take the
work, but the town will depend on imported stock. Split the labor, and the route
itself may break.

So the settlement prepares.

Pack what can move. Anchor what must remain. Choose the sheltered valley, the
ridge pass, the river road, or the old road.

Then read the loss.

What was packed? What was salvaged? What was abandoned? What was remembered?

In BANISH, loss is not a hidden fail state.

It is the shape of the next settlement.

## On-screen text

1. Ash Vale: Last Road Before Winter
2. Keep the forge. Move the work. Split the risk.
3. Prepare -> migrate -> settle -> lose visibly -> carry forward
4. Loss becomes campaign memory.
