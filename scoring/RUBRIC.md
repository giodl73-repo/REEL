# REEL rubric v0.1

REEL evaluates moving-image works across four 25-point dimensions.

| Dimension | Points | Checks |
|---|---:|---|
| Rhythm | 25 | Pacing, timing, cut logic, motion energy, scene duration, and viewer attention. |
| Emotion | 25 | Intended feeling, stakes, memory, humor, tension, desire, or explanatory satisfaction. |
| Execution | 25 | Visual craft, audio, prompts, continuity, captions, transitions, render/export quality. |
| Legibility | 25 | Audience comprehension, platform fit, phone readability, accessibility, and narrative clarity. |

Advisory threshold: below 60. Binding threshold: 60+.

This rubric is intentionally v0.1. It should evolve only from reviewed works and
logged innovations.
